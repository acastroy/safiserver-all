Setting up SafiServer: Configure the Asterisk Connection in SafiWorkshop
  


1) Connect to your SafiServer:

In the Navigator, right-click the SafiServer node and select Login SafiServer or ...

Click here to open the SafiServer Login



You should see the following popup:




2) Fill in the login form

     SafiServer IP or Hostname The IP or hostname of your SafiServer

     SafiServer Port TCP/SSH port for your SafiServer (default of 8822)

     SafiServer User Name Use sa as the login

     SafiServer Password The password you entered durring the install of your SafiServer


3) Press Login to make the connection.
